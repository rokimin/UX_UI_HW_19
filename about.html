Hello, I’m joshy Mathew

I am a budding UI/UX designer who aspires to create user-centred designs that is innovative, creative and purpose driven.

My digital design experience helps me to think creative and make solutions that are user friendly. I am curious to find simple, personal connections between brands and people. What I value the most is an opportunity to learn, team work and creative freedom as I believe that is quintessential to grow and unleash myself.

As an artist from childhood my parents nurtured my skills in being creative and I graduated with a master’s in fine arts. I have worked for various advertising agencies and brands and led their creative team in ideation and creative execution. During this journey I learned about various aspects of creative designing, typography, art direction, color theory and various aspects of digital design to effectively communicate with the audience.

However, the curiosity in me gauged my interest in UI/UX and upskilled myself through the bootcamp with University of Toronto. Currently, I am looking forward to associate with a team where I can apply my skills and learn in depth. While I am not in my official creative world I set out to my personal creative space where I enjoy doing new experiments with woodworks, paints and pencils.
